# App access and network fees — notes for Google Play (English)

**Not legal advice.** Have counsel finalize all legal documents and store listings.

## Current product model

- **App access:** one-time payment (about US$2.99) via **Stripe** unlocks **Wallet Vaval** (send, receive, and features).
- **On-chain sends:** users pay normal **network fees** (Ethereum gas, Solana, TON, etc.) to miners/validators — separate from the Stripe access fee.
